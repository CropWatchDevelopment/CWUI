## Design Context

### Users
CWUI is used in professional CropWatch dashboard workflows by growers, operators, and support staff who monitor greenhouse or cultivation environments. They need to scan status quickly, compare dated operational information, and act without losing context.

### Brand Personality
Calm, reliable, operational. The interface should feel trustworthy and workmanlike rather than playful or decorative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the existing CWUI token system and dual-theme surface language. Favor dense but readable layouts, strong separation between content regions, and responsive behavior that adapts cleanly from phone to desktop. Avoid introducing new visual gimmicks, especially gradients beyond what already exists in the shared theme tokens.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize safe, readable, maintainable UI over novelty.
2. Keep components composable: reusable shells with snippet-driven content.
3. Preserve clear information hierarchy in both dark and light themes.
4. Make scrolling, resizing, and touch interaction predictable on phone, tablet, and desktop.
5. Use shared tokens and semantic surfaces before inventing component-specific styling.
